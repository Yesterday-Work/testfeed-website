The post-and-pray problem

Brand impact cuts both ways

Content that resonates builds your brand, but posts that miss the mark or are misinterpreted can actively damage perception. Each post either builds or erodes trust with your audience.

High opportunity cost

Hours spent creating content that underperforms wastes valuable resources. Second-guessing which version to publish creates stress, delays your momentum, and leaves potential engagement on the table.

No safety net for organic content

Once you hit publish, your can't undo. Unlike ads, organic posts can't be A/B tested or gradually rolled out - you only get one chance to make the right impression.

How testfeed works

1

Audience simulation

Tell us who your target audience is, and we'll create a statistically valid simulation with thousands of unique profiles that represent your real-world audience segments.

2

Content testing

Your content is served to this virtual audience in our simulated feed (this simulates LinkedIn only for now with more platforms coming soon). Each profile reacts based on authentic platform behaviors and their unique characteristics.

3

Performance analysis

Within seconds, see projected engagement metrics, sentiment analysis, potential reactions, and identify which version of your content will perform best.

Core features

Virtual audience

Engagement metrics

Comment prediction

Frequently asked questions

What makes testfeed different from just using regular AI tools?

Unlike general AI tools that just analyze your content directly, testfeed creates an entire simulated audience and shows how they would interact with your post. This gives you much more specific insights about engagement, sentiment distribution, and potential reactions instead of generic writing suggestions. testfeed focuses exclusively on predicting audience behavior rather than just improving your writing.

How reliable are the predictions?

testfeed provides directional guidance rather than exact predictions. The simulation is designed to help you identify which version of your content will perform better and spot potential issues before publishing. While no simulation can be 100% accurate (real audience behavior depends on countless factors), our users consistently report that testfeed helps them make better content decisions and avoid problematic posts.

How do I access testfeed?

Currently, testfeed is available exclusively through ChatPost, our AI-powered content creation tool. Join our waitlist to be notified when direct access to testfeed becomes available or to learn more about using it through ChatPost today.

Can testfeed help with specific industries or niches?

Yes! testfeed creates audience simulations tailored to your specific industry and target audience. When setting up, you'll define who you're trying to reach, and testfeed will generate a proportionate sample of that audience with appropriate expertise levels, interests, and demographic profiles. This makes the predictions much more relevant than generic content analysis.

How long does it take to get results?

testfeed produces results within seconds. Simply enter your target audience description and content, and you'll quickly receive detailed engagement predictions, sentiment analysis, and even sample comments that your content might generate. This rapid feedback loop makes it practical to test multiple versions before publishing.
