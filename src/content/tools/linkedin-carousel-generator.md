## FAQ about LinkedIn Carousel Posts

### How to post a carousel on LinkedIn?
After creating your carousel, download the images and upload them as a multi-image post on LinkedIn. Select all images in order, add your caption with relevant hashtags, and publish.

### What is a LinkedIn carousel?
A LinkedIn carousel is a post format that allows you to share multiple images or slides in a single post that users can swipe through, ideal for storytelling, tutorials, or presenting multiple pieces of information.

### How to make a LinkedIn carousel post?
Use our LinkedIn Carousel Generator to transform your content into professional slides, customize the design to match your brand, and export the images to upload directly to LinkedIn as a multi-image post.

### Can you post carousels on LinkedIn?
Yes! LinkedIn supports carousel-style posts through their multi-image post feature, allowing you to upload up to 10 images that users can swipe through.

### What are the recommended LinkedIn carousel dimensions?
The optimal dimensions for LinkedIn carousel posts are 1080 x 1080 pixels (square format) with a maximum file size of 5MB per image, ensuring your content looks professional across all devices.
