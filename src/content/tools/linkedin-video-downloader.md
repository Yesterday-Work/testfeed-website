## How It Works

With testfeed's LinkedIn Video Downloader, you can easily save videos from any LinkedIn post with just a few clicks:

1. **Install Extension** Download and install our browser extension
2. **Navigate to LinkedIn** Go to any LinkedIn post containing a video
3. **Click the Icon** Click the TestFeed icon that appears on the video
4. **Choose Quality** Select your preferred download quality
5. **Save Video** Download the video directly to your device

## Key Benefits

- **Offline Access** Watch important training videos without an internet connection
- **Training Libraries** Build a collection of industry videos for team training
- **Content Creation** Easily repurpose content for marketing (with proper attribution)
- **Reliable Storage** Never lose access to important content if the original post is deleted

## Supported Video Types

- LinkedIn native videos
- LinkedIn Learning snippets
- LinkedIn Live recordings
- Embedded video content

## Usage Guidelines

Remember to respect copyright and intellectual property rights when downloading videos. Only download content for personal use or with proper permission from the content creator.
