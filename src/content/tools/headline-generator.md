## How It Works

TestFeed's Headline Generator leverages advanced AI to create headlines that resonate with your specific audience:

2. Specify your target audience and platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
3. Choose your preferred tone (professional, casual, provocative)
4. Generate multiple headline variations
5. Select and use your favorite headline with one click
